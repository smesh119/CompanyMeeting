Q3 Financials

Frank Calderoni, EVP, Operations + CFO

  • Total revenue was $524 million, our 55th consecutive quarter of growth.
  • Operating margin was 23.5% as we continued to accelerate investments back into the business.
  • Revenue growth was 21% after adjusting for currency.
  • We now have 8,300+ full-time associates and on track to hire 1,250+ net new associates in FY2016.
  • The bonus pool for Q3 was funded at $16.55M.
FY16 Goals recap

DeLisa Alexander, EVP + Chief People Officer

  • We launched the Quantum Leap project to accelerate hiring for sales and services and hired almost 300 sales and services people, a 112% increase over Q3 of last year. We closed Q3 with more than 750 hiring transactions in all departments, a 55% increase year-over-year.
  • Career Possibilities Mojo space was launched with stories and practical tips from Red Hatters who have taken opportunities to learn and grow, both within their current roles and into new ones. Check out the page and add your own tips and experiences: https://mojo.redhat.com/community/hr/careers
  • We created the Red Hat Diversity and Inclusion Advisory Group. Our diversity statistics were published on Mojo and redhat.com a little over a year ago. Since then, Red Hatters who share a passion for diversity and inclusion have connected to form this group. https://mojo.redhat.com/community/about-red-hat/we-are-red-hat/diversity
  • Beta launch of the Open Decision Making web-based training is now available. These courses are designed to help all Red Hatters practice open and inclusive decision-making: https://mojo.redhat.com/groups/open-decision-framework/blog/2015/12/14/open-decision-making-courses-now-available-online-beta
  • Chairman's Award nominations are now open. Check out the announce-list email and Mojo site and nominate someone you work with who is a role model Red Hatter. https://mojo.redhat.com/community/hr/rewards-and-recognition/chairman-s-award
  • Red Hat is sponsoring an ad campaign that is now running during the premiere of Star Wars in many different cities with print ads, online ads, and a 15-second animation that is showing in 315 theaters on 3,900 screens, with 12.2 million projected impressions. Technology roadmap

Paul Cormier, EVP + President, Products and Technology

  • We're in the middle of driving a business change in the IT industry. IT must evolve and deliver new, agile workloads while modernizing current applications.
  • The heart of our strategy is physical, virtual, private, and public clouds being used as one infrastructure by our customers, and tying everything together as one enterprise.
  • Open source is driving today's innovation, and Red Hat harnesses this to deliver mission-critical IT solutions. We're the only successful, pure open source company.
  • Two recent announcements important to our industry leadership and customer support are the strategic partnership between Red Hat and Microsoft and the acquisition of Ansible.
  • Our portfolio together is our strength and the piece that no other company can match.
CEO comments

Jim Whitehurst, President and CEO

  • “The year I joined Red Hat we had $523 million dollars in revenue, and now we're doing as much per quarter as we did per year – not far from saying we will grow that much per month.”
  • Expectations will continue to go up – this is a great opportunity to grow and step up in our careers, hone our skills, and continue to execute and elevate our game to hit these bigger numbers.
