FY16 goals recap + FY17 intro

DeLisa Alexander, EVP + Chief People Officer

FY16 GOALS RECAP
  • We saw 178% year-over-year growth in single year bookings for Red Hat Enterprise Linux OpenStack Platform. Storage bookings nearly doubled each year for the last few years, and we saw high double digit growth in middleware. Check out our press blog for more details: http://www.redhat.com/en/about/newsroom
  • We had double the customer response rate for our net promoter score survey. Customers say they are experiencing improved content findability and site performance using the Customer Portal.
  • The Red Hat Content Hub is being unveiled at Sales Kickoffs around the world. The Content Hub is a gateway that uses machine learning to help our sales teams and partners find the most relevant content across different repositories.
  • The Red Hat Associate Survey closed on March 21. We had a very high response rate this year of 76%, up 58% from last year. Results will be published in the RHAS Results group.
  • Diversity + Inclusion draft strategy. We partnered with Stanford University’s Clayman Institute and Red Hat’s Diversity and Inclusion Advisory Group to develop a draft strategy for Diversity + Inclusion. The draft is based on internal and external research, including interviews with Red Hatters and senior leaders.
  • Red Hat is at a stage where we are able to do more long-term planning, and we have a significant opportunity to align those multi-year efforts with our diversity and inclusion initiatives to make a greater impact together.
  • All Red Hatters are invited to review the draft strategy and year one roadmap, as well as the research that shaped both. Plus, check out work and activities happening across Red Hat to make our company a more diverse and inclusive place. Please share your thoughts on the draft strategy now through the end of April. We'll use your input to revise the strategy and publish an updated version.
  • The Red Hat Chairman's Award winners were announced last week. See the winners: https://mojo.redhat.com/community/hr/rewards-and-recognition/chairman-s-award

Q4 + FY16 Financials

Frank Calderoni, EVP, Operations + CFO

  • Operating margin for Q4 was 22.9%. Full fiscal year operating margin was 23.6%. Revenue growth was 21% after adjusting for currency and expense growth was 19% after adjusting for currency.
  • FY16 year over year constant currency growth billings proxy did not meet analyst expectations. Billings is one indicator of future growth, and when we are ‘priced for perfection’, any slight dip in growth indicators will concern analysts. We guided above their expectations for FY17 for revenue, earnings per share, and cash flow.
  • We are one of 13 companies in the S&P 500 who consistently posted faster revenue growth than the average company.
  • We now have 8,800+ full-time associates and currently plan to add 1200 net new hires in FY17.
  • The bonus pool for Q4 was funded at $17.9M.
